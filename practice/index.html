The perfect stop-over in between a long journey from Madurai to Kodaikanal, the silver cascade falls demand your attention while you travel. These Falls are formed as a result of the over flowing of water from man-made Kodai Lake & are the perfect spot to have a break during a long road trip. As tunning sight of water thrashing down from 180 feet right into the ground below and mesmerizing one and all with its sheer strength and beauty . One can also take a bath in the water showers of the falls provided the temperature is right As falls site is slightly off from main road, one can easily relax , take photographs etc. & also enjoy some cool drinks available at site during tourist season.
